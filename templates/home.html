Potato Leaf Disease Prediction

Welcome to CropGuard!

CropGuard is a web tool that helps you identify potential diseases in your potato plants by analyzing uploaded leaf images.

This tool is designed to be a simple and user-friendly way for farmers and gardeners to monitor the health of their potato crops.

Benefits of using CropGuard

  • Early detection of potential diseases
  • Improved decision-making for crop management
  • Increased crop yields
  • Reduced reliance on chemical pesticides
